The first Web Nomads Asia will be at Phuket Graceland Resort & Spa in Thailand.

How to get there

The airport nearest to the venue is Phuket International Airport (HKT). You can fly directly there or change in Suvarnabhumi Airport (BKK) in Bangkok which is an hour away.
